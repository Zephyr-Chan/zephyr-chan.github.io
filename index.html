About

I am an undergraduate student at Wuyi University, School of Electronic and Information Engineering, majoring in Software Engineering. My research interests lie at the intersection of AI Agent, Large Language Models (LLM), Diffusion Models, and Brain-Computer Interface (BCI).

Currently, I am leading two core research projects. The first involves developing a multimodal AI Agent for automated radiotherapy treatment planning, with the goal of building an intelligent system capable of clinical decision-making to optimize the radiotherapy workflow. The second focuses on large model approaches for EEG signals, aiming to improve decoding accuracy and enable cross-subject transfer of brain-computer interface systems.

I plan to pursue an MPhil degree in Computer Science to deepen my research capabilities and contribute to the forefront of artificial intelligence. I am actively seeking academic collaboration opportunities -- if you are interested in any form of collaboration or technical exchange, please feel free to reach out via email or instant messaging.

Additionally, I maintain academic and personal growth columns on Zhihu and Xiaohongshu, where I share insights and references for learners and researchers in related fields.
